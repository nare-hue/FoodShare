How It Works

  • Donate Leftover Food: Simply sign up and start donating your food. The food is then collected and redistributed.
  • Earn Points: For each donation, you earn points that can be redeemed for rewards.
  • Our Network: We partner with homes, restaurants, and businesses to make the process seamless.
