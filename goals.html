Goals

Food Share contributes to SDG 2 by redistributing surplus food to address hunger and food insecurity. It ensures equitable access to nutritious meals through efficient collection, preparation, and delivery systems, reducing food waste. By leveraging technology and community engagement, it creates a sustainable solution to eradicate hunger and promote food security.


Food Share supports UAE Vision 2031 by promoting sustainability through reduced food waste and eco-friendly practices. It fosters innovation with AI-driven analytics and advanced delivery technologies while enhancing social development through community engagement. The initiative strengthens economic diversification and builds a knowledge-driven society by involving students and volunteers
